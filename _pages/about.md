I am a Math & Computer Science Teacher at SundarSTEM School, where I teach competitive programming along with the O-level Mathematics Syllabus D. Previously, I have worked in different roles, such as Machine Learning Engineer, Teaching Assistant for graduate and undergraduate courses. As a Research Engineer at Knowledge Platform, I developed a language checker specialized for the English of non-native writers. I did my MS (CS) at SEECS NUST and my BS (CS) at FAST NUCES Islamabad. I specialize in deep learning for computer vision, and have a keen interest in Medical Imaging, Bioinformatics, and Document Understanding, especially for Arabic scripts. I work diligently and have a keen interest in learning new concepts, tools, and techniques. I have good mathematical, analytical, and logical problem-solving skills. I am always open to new opportunities to grow and contribute to advancements in Computer Science for the improvement of life on Earth.
